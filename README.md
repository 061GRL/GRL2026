Datasets of "The uniqueness of the full magnetic/gravity gradient tensor" .
The presented datasets are used in the paper submitted to Geophysical Research Letter. They will be public when the paper is in publication. 
The dataset includes magnetic anomalies and the magnetic inversions presented in the manuscript, the details are shown blew.

Part 1: Magnetic anomalies data ("DataS1.mat", and "DataS2.mat")
Two datasets in Matlab format are uploaded with the names of "DataS1.mat" and "DataS2.mat". 
(1) The "DataS1.mat", used in Figure S1, includes the modeled magnetic responses of total field anomaly (TFA) and magnetic gradient tensor components (Txx, Txy, Txz, Tyy, Tyz) with a data spacing of 2 m.
(2) The "DataS2.mat", used in Figure 2, includes the field magnetic tensor anomalies (Txx, Txy, Txz, Tyy, Tyz) and the total field anomaly (TFA) with a grid spacing of 220 m.
